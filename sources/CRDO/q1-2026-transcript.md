# Q1 2026 Earnings Transcript: Credo Technology Group (CRDO)
**Date:** May 14, 2026

## Key Highlights
- **AEC Dominance:** Active Electrical Cables (AEC) for 800G and 1.6T systems seeing record adoption in Blackwell-based clusters.
- **Power Efficiency:** New "HiWire" solutions reducing interconnect power consumption by 20%, a critical factor as DC power limits are reached.
- **Guidance:** Q2 revenue guidance raised 15% above consensus.
- **CEO Quote:** "As AI models scale, the bottleneck is moving from the GPU to the interconnect. Our low-power, high-speed connectivity is essential for the thermal management of 2026-era data centers."
